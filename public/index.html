Welcome to Space Education API

Explore the wonders of space and learn about celestial bodies, stars, galaxies, missions, phenomena, and more!

API Endpoints

Discover fascinating information about space through our APIs:

  • GET /api/planets: Get details about planets in our solar system.

  • GET /api/stars: Learn about the most famous stars in the universe.

  • GET /api/galaxies: Explore galaxies and their characteristics.

  • GET /api/missions: Learn about the most famous space missions.

  • GET /api/phenomenas: Explore interesting phenomenas in universe.

  • GET /api/terminologies: Learn about the important terminologies about the universe.
